This is a multiplayer game inspired by Google's Moon Phase Doodle.

  • Take turns placing moon phase cards on the board.
  • Score points by placing cards adjacent to other cards. Get one point for placing a phase next to an identical phase. Score two points for placing a phase next to another phase where the two add up to a full moon. Score one point per phase for 3 or more phases next to each other that are consecutive in the lunar cycle.
