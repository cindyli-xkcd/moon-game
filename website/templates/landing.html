This is a multiplayer game inspired by the Google Doodle: Rise of the Half Moon.

  • Take turns placing moon phase cards on the board.
  • Score points by placing cards adjacent to other cards.
    • Score one point for placing a phase next to an identical phase.
    • Score two points for placing a phase next to another phase where the two add up to a full moon.
    • Score one point per phase for 3 or more phases next to each other that are consecutive in the lunar cycle.

    More features like decks and wildcards coming soon.
